About Us

Healing Through Excellence

Maadhuri College of Physiotherapy was established in 2023 with a vision to create world-class physiotherapy professionals who combine clinical expertise with compassionate care.

Located in the holy city of Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, our institution is committed to providing quality education and practical training that prepares students for rewarding careers in physiotherapy.

🎯
Our Mission

To provide comprehensive physiotherapy education that combines theory with hands-on clinical practice.

🌟
Our Vision

To be the leading physiotherapy college in Andhra Pradesh, recognized for academic excellence.

Bachelor of Physiotherapy

BPT

A 4.5-year undergraduate program (4 years + 6 months internship) covering all aspects of physiotherapy including musculoskeletal, neurological, cardiopulmonary, and pediatric physiotherapy.

  • Duration: 4 Years + 6 Months Internship
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Biology
  • Admissions Open for 2025
  • Counselling Code: MMAA
